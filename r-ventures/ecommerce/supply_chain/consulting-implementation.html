Designing a global solution template with localizations to meet the specific business requirements for each geography: India, Thailand, RSA, Brazil, Argentina

Problems

- Sub-optimal truck utilization with manual truck load building
- No centralized platform to monitor all logistics activities across multiple geographies
- Dock congestion leading to poor TAT and dock balancing conflicts
- Lack of real time communication across stakeholders
- Lack of visibility and order into trips and order statuses
- Data issues due to single source of truth
- Outsourced logistics planning and operations

Solutions

- One centralized platform for inbound movements management
- Dynamic planning for improved consolidation
- 3D load-building for better utilization & LCPU improvement
- Transport requirements forecasting capability for budgeting and scenario modeling
- Aggregation of GPS feeds for real-time visibility into material availability at plants
- Automation of planning, collaboration, analysis
