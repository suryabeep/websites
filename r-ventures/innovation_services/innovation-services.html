Innovation Services

Thoughts about Innovation Services

Our goal is to democratize Innovation in a structured way for our clients so that they are totally aware of various technology innovations happening across the globe in the domain of retail.
Our job is to engage with the best technology VCs, PE organizations and incubators to identify such innovations which has the potential to scale up and move the needle on sales, gross margin, cost and customer experience. Every single day “Digital entrepreneurs” born with a concept-dream backed by groundbreaking technology. Google started its journey in a garage!

It may not be feasible for you as a business entity to keep track of everchanging technology landscape courtesy this start up ecosystems across the globe. Many of these startups offer bleeding edge technology which may be able to solve a very niche biz problem for you. We act as “Aggregator” by constantly scanning this eco-system and brining you the relevant technologies for your business and create a virtuous loop so that technology does not become monopoly of big retail. We organize Innovation day, hackathon and set up and manage “Innovation garage” for you.
