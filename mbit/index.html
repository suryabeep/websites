Welcome to MBit Wireless

Powering mobile broadband

MBit Wireless is developing solution for mobile broadband communications. Our solution enables broadband access on the move, anytime and anywhere. Our solution enables our customers to develop cost-effective mobile broadband products for global markets.

Our founding and management team has two decades of individual industry experience in developing and commercializing chips and software for cellular handset market and launched products for Tier-1 OEMs.

We are in stealth mode of operation and for further details please contact us at info@mbitwireless.com.

Company

MBit Wireless is founded by executives with extensive experience in the wireless semiconductor industry. Our management team has two decades of individual industry experience in developing and commercializing chips and software for mass market and launched products for Tier-1 OEMs.

We are developing system and software solution for mobile broadband communications.

Technology

MBit Wireless has established a strong foundation in systems, advanced signal processing and algorithms, protocol and platform software, integration, test and product engineering to develop system solutions for next generation mobile broadband technologies.

Our technology has resulted in significant achievements in the mobile broadband industry.

Products

MMBit Wireless has developed solution based on patented technology for mobile broadband communications applications. We combine our expertise in digital communications systems and embedded software to develop system solutions. Our products offer seamless mobility, ultra low power, robustness and high performance.
